### Launched the Beta Version of the Project Estimation Agent

We’ve deployed [Smart Project Estimation](https://iproject.iconicompany.com/) (Beta). It’s a tool designed to help you quickly structure requirements and estimate project scope.

---

**How It Works**

Upload a project description (PDF, DOCX, or plain text), and the agent generates a task list and effort estimates by role in one click. If the results look good, you can send the application directly to us for a detailed review.

---

**Accuracy and AI**

The analysis is performed by a language model, so the estimation is preliminary. It’s not a replacement for a full technical specification, but it’s a great way to quickly understand the "ballpark figures" and complexity at the start.

---

**Team and Feedback**

We look forward to your feedback! And if you need more than just an estimate — like building a team and launching the project — we’re here to help.

---

### Why Speed Outweighs Discussions

Most teams lose weeks before the first line of code is even written. They discuss, pick the "perfect" stack, and debate: "Maybe Vue?", "Let's compare again." While they argue, others are already shipping MVPs.

We’ve long used a proven model: **Next.js · TypeScript · Bun · Elysia · Postgres**. Not because it’s the "perfect stack," but because it’s where we work the fastest.

Plus, we have ready-to-use patterns:
- Auth / RBAC
- Admin panels and APIs
- Observability
- Pre-configured CI/CD

Projects don't start from scratch. And yes, AI helps here too: writing boilerplate, generating types, speeding up reviews, and parsing logs.

The biggest market shift right now isn't just the existence of AI — it's the fact that the speed gap between teams has become x5–x10. Whoever tests hypotheses faster wins.
