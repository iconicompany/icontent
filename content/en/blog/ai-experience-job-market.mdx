Tired of competing with a thousand frontend developers or analysts?

Bad news: it's only going to get worse.
Good news: there's a simple way out.

---

## Stop being "just another candidate"

Become a candidate with AI experience.

You don't necessarily need to build complex systems.

It's enough to have:

- projects with OpenAI / LLM
- task automation
- simple assistants

But what's especially valued is:

👉 **experience in agentic systems**
(where AI doesn't just respond, but executes processes)

---

## Real Case Study

Recently, a specialist received an invitation for a vacancy as an analyst with AI experience.

They:

1. registered with us
2. adapted their resume
3. specified the missing AI experience

But an error occurred.

Our system added skills, but **didn't detail this experience within the projects**.

---

## What Happened Next

- we sent a "raw" resume
- received a clarification request
- started refining

⏳ Lost time.

And the candidate?

While we were correcting the resume, they had already **received an offer through another contractor**.

---

## The Conclusion is Simple

- If you have AI experience - **companies start competing for you**
- If not - **you compete with thousands of candidates**

The market has already changed.

The only question is when you'll accept it.
