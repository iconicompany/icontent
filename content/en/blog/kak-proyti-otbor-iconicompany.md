You apply for jobs - and you don't understand why you're not getting replies. I'll explain how it works for us.

We don't send out job offers to just anyone. We only send proposals to specialists who have a **skill fit ≥ 85%**.

Now for an important point:

👉 A job description might have **10-20 requirements**

👉 And it's not about "generally fits," but rather **having experience for each key requirement**

It's not "similar in tech stack." It's about **actually having done what the business needs**.

## What Happens Next

Our agent 👤 **Svetlana** reviews your resume line by line:

*   checks experience
*   matches against requirements
*   verifies depth (not just keywords)

If there are enough matches - she writes to you.

## But There's a Problem

To write to you, we need contact information.

📌 If your resume doesn't include Telegram → you simply won't receive a message even if you're a perfect fit

## What You Need to Do

1.  ✅ Add your **Telegram to your "About Me" section**

2.  ✅ Subscribe to our bot
    → to receive job openings **in the first minute after they're posted**

3.  ✅ Or simply send your resume to the bot:
    👉 [https://t.me/iconicompanybot](https://t.me/iconicompanybot)

After this:

*   ⚡ you'll be placed in a priority queue
*   ⚡ you'll receive **targeted offers tailored to your profile**
*   ⚡ and learn about relevant job openings before others

The hiring market is changing. It's not those who "seem to fit" who succeed, but those with **proven experience for specific tasks**.

💡 If you're one of them - we'll find you. But give us a way to contact you.
