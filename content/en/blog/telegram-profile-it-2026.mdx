Recruiters are working massively through Telegram. If your profile is not ready there, you are invisible.

We'll break down how to ensure you are found quickly.

## Why Telegram is everything

Previously, recruiters wrote via email or by phone. Now, searches are increasingly conducted directly in TG:

1. The recruiter searches for you by phone number in Telegram.
2. Didn't find you - searches by name or email in TG.
3. Didn't find you - the candidate is lost, the vacancy goes to someone else.

Your Telegram profile is now your **digital business card** and the main entry point for a recruiter. It's a work tool, not a social network.
