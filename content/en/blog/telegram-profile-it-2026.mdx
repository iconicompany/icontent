Recruiters are massively working through Telegram. If your profile isn't ready there, you're invisible.

We'll break down how to ensure you're found quickly.

## Why Telegram is Key

Recruiters used to write emails or call. Now, searches are increasingly happening directly on TG:

1. The recruiter searches for you by phone number on Telegram.
2. If not found, they search by name or email on TG.
3. If not found, the candidate is lost, and the vacancy goes to someone else.

Your Telegram profile is now your **digital business card** and the main entry point for a recruiter. It's a work tool, not a social network.
