🚀 As of today, Iconicompany is an official participant of [HICOOL 2026](https://www.hicool.com/)!

We are entering the prestigious international arena with our project for an **autonomous AI operator for forming engineering teams**.

## Why is this important now?

According to a fresh analysis by Sequoia Capital ("Services: The New Software"), the recruiting and staffing market is the largest segment of service industries, valued at **over $200 billion**.

Key insights from experts:

- 🔹 The **top of the funnel** (sourcing, screening, matching, and outreach) is "pure intelligence" that can and should be automated.
- 🔹 The **bottom of the funnel** (cultural fit assessment and deal closing) currently remains within the realm of human judgment.

## Our Stance: "Autopilot Wedge"

At Iconicompany, we are entering the so-called "Autopilot wedge". Our system doesn't just assist the recruiter (Copilot) - it **replaces the entire "turnkey" process**, delivering a ready-made engineering team in **48 hours** using an **Outcome-as-a-Service** model.

We stand alongside global industry leaders - **Juicebox**, **Mercor**, and **Jack & Jill**, who are already rewriting the rules of the game in the global talent market.

## Where are we headed?

We are moving towards creating a new labor market infrastructure where engineering resources are assembled as quickly and reliably as software code! 🤖🏗️
