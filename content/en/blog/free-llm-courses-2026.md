Want to get into AI but don't know where to start?
I've put together a selection of free courses on LLMs and Generative AI that genuinely provide a solid foundation (and more 👇).

---

## 1. LLM Fundamentals

- **Introduction to Large Language Models**
[coursera.org/learn/introduction-to-large-language-models](https://www.coursera.org/learn/introduction-to-large-language-models)

- **Introduction to LLMs** (Google Cloud, Udacity)
[udacity.com/course/introduction-large-language-models-google-cloud](https://www.udacity.com/course/introduction-large-language-models-google-cloud--cd12959)

- **Master Large Language Models** (DataCamp)
[datacamp.com/courses/large-language-models-llms-concepts](https://www.datacamp.com/courses/large-language-models-llms-concepts)

[datacamp.com/courses/large-language-models-for-business](https://www.datacamp.com/courses/large-language-models-for-business)

## 💡 My Advice

Don't go through everything indiscriminately. Take a combination:

1. **Fundamentals** - LLM intro
2. **Prompt engineering**
3. **Python / practice**

And apply it immediately - otherwise, you'll forget everything in a week.
